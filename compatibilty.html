Aries (March 21 - April 19)

Most Compatible

  • Aries is most compatible with fellow fire signs Leo and Sagittarius because they share similar energy, enthusiasm, and passion for life.
  • Gemini, an air sign, complements Aries with their intellectual stimulation and adaptability.

Less Compatible

  • Aries may find it challenging to connect with Cancer's emotional depth and desire for security.
  • Capricorn's practicality and reserved nature can clash with Aries' impulsive and adventurous spirit.
